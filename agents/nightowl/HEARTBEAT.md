# HEARTBEAT.md

Per-heartbeat checklist. Keep small.

## Every poll

- Any in-flight tasks? If yes, check `gh pr checks` for the PRs you opened.
- Any clones in `repos/` older than 7 days? `trash` them.

## Once a day

- Skim `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md` from the past few days. Anything worth promoting to `MEMORY.md`?

## Stay quiet (HEARTBEAT_OK) when

- No active task and nothing changed since last check.
- It's been &lt;30 min since last check.
- It's between 23:00–08:00 IST and nothing is on fire.
